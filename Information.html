About Us:

This site is maintained by the Calgary Association of Aircraft Enthusiasts. The aim is to share news and interesting historical facts with current and potential memebers. As an organization we arrange group tours, swap meets and a yearly banquet for the airplane enthusiasts of Calgary. Below is a table listing the tiers of membership with the fees and benefits one recieves with each. As well there is a form for contacting us with membership requests and general inquiries. Feel free to fill it out and the secretary will get back to you as soon as possible.
